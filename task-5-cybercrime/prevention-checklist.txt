Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Remember: entering UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving
- Always verify the identity of the person before making payments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
- Use only official and trusted payment apps
- Enable SMS and app notifications for transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 for quick assistance
